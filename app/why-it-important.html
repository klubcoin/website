Secure your Wallet

When you start using your Klub Coin wallet, you will receive a random set of 12 words. This is also referred to as your Recovery Phrase. It is a key element when using a hardware wallet and must be kept safe and offline at all times.

WHY A RECOVERY PHRASE?

Recovery phrases, also called seed phrases, are absolutely essential when using a hardware wallet. If your app or phone becomes unusable for any reason (theft or destruction), you have not lost your precious cryptocurrencies. Your 12 words serve as a backup for all cryptocurrencies managed through your device.

Let us say your phone is lost and you want to have access to your cryptocurrencies again. When you start your new device, you will have the option to select "Restore from Recovery Phrase". After you enter all 12 words correctly, your device will give you access to the exact same addresses as before!

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Your recovery phrase can be considered as a master key. As you may have already guessed, there is a specific list of words that can be part of your recovery phrase. There are 2048 words in total in this list, which is also called the BIP-39 word list.

If you are worried that someone might guess your 12 words, do not worry. There is a huge total of 5444517870735015415413993718908291383296 combinations!!! You're more likely to win the lottery every month for the rest of your life.

Even though your recovery phrase is presented to you as a series of words, it is actually a long string of data that looks similar to a cryptocurrency address. For your convenience, these are displayed as human-readable words.

KEEP IT SECRET & SAFE!

As mentioned earlier, your 12-word recovery phrase is a backup for all your crypto wallet. This also means that anyone who gets their hands on your 12 words can access your cryptocurrencies. Therefore, it is extremely important that you keep your Recovery Phrase safe.

The following steps can help you do that:

  • Never share your 12-word recovery phrase with anyone
  • Never enter your recovery phrase into a computer, phone or any other internet-connected device. If your computer would get hacked, the attacker would be able to access your 12 words.
  • Only keep your recovery phrase on a piece of paper or metal backup.

Making sure to follow these simple rules will help keep your wallet and cryptocurrencies safe.

ALWAYS DOUBLE CHECK

Apart from keeping your recovery phrase safe, it is also important that you have your 12 words written down correctly. Any mistake in spelling or word order would result in a different set of addresses being generated and would not restore your precious assets.
